Our Story

Bringing Authentic Indian Flavors to Edenderry

Welcome to Zaffran

Established in 2020, Zaffran Restaurant has become Edenderry's premier destination for authentic Indian cuisine. Located in the heart of County Offaly, we pride ourselves on bringing the rich, diverse flavors of India to the Irish Midlands.

Our name, "Zaffran," means saffron in Hindi - the golden spice that represents luxury, flavor, and tradition. Just like this precious ingredient, we believe in offering nothing but the finest quality in every dish we serve.

From traditional curries and tandoori specialties to innovative fusion dishes, our menu celebrates the culinary heritage of India while embracing local Irish ingredients and preferences.

Restaurant Image

Our Culinary Team

Our experienced chefs bring decades of combined experience from various regions of India. Trained in traditional cooking methods and armed with family recipes passed down through generations, they create magic in our kitchen every day.

We use only the freshest ingredients, authentic spices imported directly from India, and traditional cooking techniques including our clay tandoor oven. Each dish is prepared with care, ensuring that every bite transports you to the streets of Delhi, the beaches of Goa, or the palaces of Rajasthan.
